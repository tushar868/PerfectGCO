Valve Tappet

A valve tappet, also referred to as a cam follower, is a small component within an engine. Unlike some other parts, it lacks a roller to track the camshaft lobe. Its primary function is to convert the rotational motion of the camshaft into vertical motion, thereby activating the intake or exhaust valve. This crucial role ensures proper valve timing and efficient engine performance.


  • key points about valve tappets

    • Function: Valve tappets serve as intermediaries between each camshaft lobe and the valve lifting mechanism.
    • Size: The size of a valve tappet varies based on the specific engine design.
    • Material: These components are meticulously crafted from high-quality raw materials, ensuring durability and optimal performance.
