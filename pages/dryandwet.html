Dry and Wet Liner

We are prime manufacturer, specializing in Centrifugal Cylinder Liners, Sleeves, and Air-Cooled Blocks. All Cylinder liner, sleeves are manufactures in gray cast iron with special chemical compositions, Air Cooled Cylinder Liners are made from special “Shell Moulding Process” having wear resisting close grained iron casting to ensure long life and efficient cooling. Commitment to excellence and capacity to produce 150 thousand pieces of cylinder liner per month positions us as a trusted and reliable player in Global supply chain for aftermarket automotive parts.
