About Us

Welcome to Perfect Group Co. - Your Trusted Partner in Automotive spares. We specialize in manufacturing and exporting high-quality automotive parts worldwide. Our extensive product range includes Cylinder Liners, Sleeves, Air-Cooled Blocks, Engine valves, Valve Guide, Valve Seat, Piston, Connecting Rods, Crankshaft and custom-made casting parts, ensuring we meet the diverse needs of our customers.

Mission

To deliver top quality engine parts at affordable prices.

Vision

To become your one stop shop for all your engine parts need.

Values

Justice, fair play, integrity, respect, far sightedness and sincerity

Quality is paramount to us, and our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities and rigorous quality control processes ensure that each product meets the highest standards. We adhere to both internal quality protocols and external regulatory requirements, providing our customers with confidence in our products. Through strategic partnerships with B-to-B businesses, distributors, and automotive workshops globally, we deliver our products efficiently, fostering long-term relationships. PERFECT GROUP CO is one of the leading engine parts manufacturers & exporters, serving automotive OEMs and aftermarket customers around the world. We have been manufacturing and exporting components for automotive engine parts systems under the "PERFECT SPARES" brand. We have a well-qualified and motivated team of 111 members at your service, and are ISO / TS 16949: 2009 certified for manufacturing of cylinder liners and marketing of automotive components.

Our state-of-the-art facilities encompass an in-house casting foundry and a meticulously equipped machine shop boasting with CNC, VMC, and specialized machinery like boring, honing, grinding, and custom-made lathes, ensuring precision in every aspect of production to meet our customers' exacting standards. Committed to excellence, we provide our Quality Control team with the finest tools, including an on-site spectrometer, empowering them to maintain stringent chemical compositions and meet the precise specifications set forth by our esteemed clientele. At Perfect Spares, we pride ourselves on delivering superior products crafted with expertise, innovation, and unwavering dedication to customer satisfaction.
